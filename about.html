About us

Empowering Business with Odoo

In the dynamic field of enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, ITSS has firmly established itself as a beacon of innovation and excellence. Since its foundation in 2017, ITSS has rapidly gained recognition as a gold partner of Odoo, distinguishing itself through a comprehensive suite of ERP solutions that empower businesses to optimize their operations.

ITSS partnership with Odoo reflects its commitment to delivering cutting-edge, customizable solutions that meet the unique needs of each client, whether in retail, manufacturing, or services.

ITSS Background and Milestones

ITSS was established with a vision to transform the way businesses operate through advanced ERP solutions. Since its inception, the company has not only met its initial goals but has also set new benchmarks in the ERP industry. Here’s a closer look at ITSS's journey:

  1. Foundation 2017

    ITSS was established, embarking on a mission to redefine the landscape of enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. The company's founding marked the start of a journey focused on delivering superior ERP systems to businesses across various industries in KSA and Egypt. From its inception, ITSS has been committed to providing comprehensive, customizable solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client, helping businesses streamline their operations and achieve sustainable growth. Through its innovative approach to ERP implementation, customization, integration, and support,

    ITSS has empowered organizations to optimize their workflows, enhance productivity, and stay competitive in today's competitive marketplace. This foundational year laid the groundwork for ITSS's subsequent achievements and growth, cementing its reputation as a trusted partner in the ERP industry. The company's success since then reflects its dedication to quality, excellence, and customer satisfaction, which remain at the core of its mission today.

  2. Rapid Certification

    ITSS's rapid ascent in the enterprise resource planning (ERP) industry is marked by its impressive certification achievements. Within just six months of its founding, ITSS earned gold partner status with Odoo, a testament to its exceptional project delivery and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. This rapid certification reflects the company's focus on delivering high-quality, tailored solutions that address the specific needs of its clients. By seamlessly implementing and customizing Odoo's ERP systems

    ITSS has empowered businesses across various sectors to streamline their operations, and achieve sustainable growth. The company's ability to attain this prestigious certification in such a short period speaks volumes about its expertise, dedication, and proficiency in managing complex ERP projects. This early milestone has solidified ITSS's position as a leader in the ERP industry, setting a strong foundation for its continued success and innovation.

  3. Expansion

    ITSS's growth is characterized by remarkable expansion, reflecting its ability to deliver top-tier ERP solutions across diverse industries. The company has successfully completed over 40 ERP projects, demonstrating its expertise in managing complex implementations and delivering high-quality solutions customized to each client's needs. This track record has led to the onboarding of over 2,000 users, providing businesses with the tools they need. Additionally, ITSS has initiated significant training programs for fresh graduates,

    contributing to the professional development of the next generation of ERP experts. These initiatives not only reflect ITSS's dedication to nurturing talent but also its commitment to expanding its capabilities and staying at the forefront of the ERP industry. In the future, ITSS aims to broaden its reach by expanding its services across the Middle East and MENA region, bringing its innovative ERP solutions to businesses in these dynamic markets as we recognize the potential for growth and development in these regions.
